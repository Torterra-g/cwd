Intro

In the world of Pokemon many adventures can be had. But what pokemon are the best options to travel along with you. Now everyone has their favorites but some pokemon do perform better in battle than some others. Today we will be exploring what kinds of pokemon are the best to bring with you on your journey.

Stats

Now when it comes to the Pokemon games. Stat spread is really important. Some pokemon get a really good stat spread that makes them unstoppable. While others can get a really poor stat spread. You will find that legendaires and mythicals have some of the best stat spreads of all pokemon. But that's an easy guess. A three stage evolution line typically develops higher stats and brings more power.
